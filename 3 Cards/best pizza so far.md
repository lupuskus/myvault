On 21/5

Dough from Ooni cookbook:
for 2 bigger or 3 smaller pizzas
300g strong bread flour (Morrisons)
180g water (60%)
1 1/2 tsp yeast
1 1/2 tsp salt

Mix long enough. Check if gluten has developed by stretching it.

1 hour bulk rising
then put in boxes and keep at different temp
