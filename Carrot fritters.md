[[Fritters]]
## INGREDIENTS
- 3 medium carrots (try sweet potatoe, parsnips, squash) grated
- 90  g (mls) gram flour
- ½  teaspoon baking powder
- cumin, turmeric, paprika 
- Pinch chilli powder
- 120  ml  water
- 2-4 tablespoons parsley or coriander
- Salt
## INSTRUCTIONS
- mix, fry in pan or air fry
- Make big batch for freezer
